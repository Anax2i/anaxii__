This is the forum place where all beatmaps uploaded to osu! resides.

How to use the forums
---------------------

**Log-in to the osu! website first.** Then, go to osu!website's menu "Community;Forums" and scroll down to search for it or click the hyperlink: [Beatmap forum](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/12).

Directory of the Beatmaps forum
-------------------------------

-   [Beatmaps](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/12) - Beatmap stuff goes here.
    -   [Ranked/Approved Beatmaps](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/14) - ***Officially approved beatmaps reside in here.***
    -   [Pending Beatmaps](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/6) - ***Beatmaps that are usually complete but pending approval.***
    -   [Works In Progress/Help](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/10) - ***Post your work-in-progress beatmaps, or find general help in here!***
        -   [General Questions](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/56)
        -   [Storyboarding](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/20) - ***Discuss storyboarding, request help, and share your WIPs.***
        -   [Modding Queues](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/60)
    -   [Beatmap Graveyard](http://osu.ppy.sh/forum/19) - ***Beatmaps that haven't been active for 4 weeks or more will be moved here.***
    -   [Beatmap Projects](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/53) - ***Announce, discuss and participate in beatmapping projects here.***
