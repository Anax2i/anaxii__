# Death Stream

**Death Streams** are an very intense kind of [Streams](/wiki/Beatmap/Pattern/osu!/Stream). They occur in Expert difficulties and test the players ability in clicking to the rythm in higher speeds than usual for longer periods.

**Death Streams** tend to last longer than 30 [hit circles](/wiki/Gameplay/Hit_object/Hit_circle).
