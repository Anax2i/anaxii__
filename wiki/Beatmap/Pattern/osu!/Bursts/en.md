# Bursts

A **burst** is a pattern where three or more [hit circles](/wiki/Gameplay/Hit_object/Hit_circle) are [stacked](/wiki/Beatmapping/Mapping_techniques/Stack) on top of each other in 1/4 or faster ticks on the [timeline](/wiki/Client/Beatmap_editor/Timelines#hit-objects).

Bursts can also start and end [streams](/wiki/Beatmap/Pattern/Stream)
