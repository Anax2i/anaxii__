# Bursts

**Bursts** are a pattern where three or more [hit circles](/wiki/Gameplay/Hit_object/Hit_circle) are [stacked](/wiki/Beatmapping/Mapping_techniques/Stack) on top of eachother in 1/4 or faster ticks.
