# Doubles

![](img/double.jpg "An visual example of a double in the osu! gamemode.")

A **Double** pattern is when 2 [hit circles](/wiki/Gameplay/Hit_object/Hit_circle) are [stacked](/wiki/Beatmapping/Mapping_techniques/Stack) on each other in a 1/2 or faster rythm spacing.
