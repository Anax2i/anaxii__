The forums are a collection of threads on different topics as a medium for community discussion and can be acessed through the community dropdown menu on the main site.
The forum index is generalised using categories with sub-categories under them. Can be found [here](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums)

The main categories are:
**Osu!:** for discussion of topic relating to the game [osu!](/wiki/Disambiguation/osu!)
**Beatmaps:** for discussion of [beatmaps](/wiki/Beatmap) and related topics like [beatmapping](/wiki/Beatmapping)
**Other:** for discussion of topics that do not fall into the other categories
**Language Specific:** for discussion in languages other than english
