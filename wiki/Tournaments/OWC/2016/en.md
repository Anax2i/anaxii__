osu! World Cup 2016
============

![osu! World Cup 2017](logo.png)

The **osu! World Cup 2016** (_**OWC 2016**_) is a country-based osu! tournament hosted by the osu! team](/wiki/People/The_Team). It is the 7th installment of the osu! World Cup.
