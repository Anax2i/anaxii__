# osu! Philippines Nationals 2021 - osu!mania 4K/7K

![PHNM2021 banner](img/banner.png)

The **osu! Philippines Nationals 2021 (osu!mania) 4K/7K** (***PHNM 2021***) was a one-on-one, osu!mania tournament made for osu! players based in the Philippines or of Filipino Heritage.
