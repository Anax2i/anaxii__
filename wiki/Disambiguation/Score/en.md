# Score (disambiguation)

**Score** can have multiple meanings:

- Score submission, a player's individual result on a [beatmap](/wiki/Beatmap)
- [Ranked score](/wiki/Score/Ranked_score), the total sum of the best results for all [ranked](/wiki/Beatmap/Category#ranked) difficulties played online
- [Total score](/wiki/Score/Total_score), the sum of all results on all beatmaps, including failed attempts
- The [scoring algorithm](/wiki/Score) itself
