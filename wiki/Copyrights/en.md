What is *copyright*?
====================

For reference to copyright, refer to this page [Wikipedia on *Copyright*](https://en.wikipedia.org/?title=Copyright).

In short terms, quote from Wikipedia,

  
"*Copyright is a legal right created by the law of a country that grants the creator of an original work exclusive rights to its use and distribution, usually for a limited time. The exclusive rights are not absolute; they are limited by limitations and exceptions to copyright law, including fair use.*"

What is *Creative Commons*?
===========================

For reference to Creative Commons (or CC for short), refer to this page [Wikipedia on *Creative Commons*](https://en.wikipedia.org/?title=Creative_Commons).

In short terms,

  
The *Creative Commons* \[*Attribution*\] allows content creators to create and legally communicate (with a free licence) to share content amongst others. In some cases, for commercial-use and with modification without reference to its original creator, in others non-commercial-use without modification with reference to its original creator.<sup>\[Citation\\ Needed\]</sup> This will vary per given licence (from the original creator, of course)<sup>\[Citation\\ Needed\]</sup>
