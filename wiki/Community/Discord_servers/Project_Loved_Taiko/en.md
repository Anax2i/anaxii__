**Project Loved: Taiko** is a community [Discord](https://discord.com) server designated for osu!taiko players and mappers alike looking for information about Project Loved or to discuss and suggest maps for consideration. Users are able to directly interact with the osu!taiko Loved Captains, other Project Loved Team members, and view Captain discussions publicly.
