**osu!catch Project Loved** is a community [Discord](https://discord.com) server designated for osu!catch players and mappers alike looking for information about Project Loved or to discuss and suggest maps for consideration. Users are able to directly interact with the osu!catch Loved Captains and other Project Loved Team members.
