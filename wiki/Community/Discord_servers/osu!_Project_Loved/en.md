**osu! Project Loved** is a community [Discord](https://discord.com) server designated for osu! players and mappers alike looking for information about Project Loved or to discuss and suggest maps for consideration. Users are able to directly interact with the osu! Loved Captains and other Project Loved Team members.

## Verification

In order to gain access to the channels, users must verify their identity with their osu! accounts using [AxerBot](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/1604925).
