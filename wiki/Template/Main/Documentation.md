### Usage

<!-- -->

    {{Main
    |Article 1
    }}

You may also include additional links, up to ten in total:

    {{Main
    |Article 1
    |Article 2
    |Article 3
    |Article 4
    |Article 5
    |Article 6
    |Article 7
    |Article 8
    |Article 9
    |Article 10
    }}

For each link, you may optionally specify a link appearance (this is for articles where a piped link would be used). Note that the extra parameters use a lower case 'L', eg. l1 <u>not</u> 11:

    {{Main
    |Article 1
    |Article 2
    |Article 3
    |Article 4
    |Article 5
    |Article 6
    |Article 7
    |Article 8
    |Article 9
    |Article 10
    |l1=Article 1 link appearance
    |l2=Article 2 link appearance
    |l3=Article 3 link appearance
    |l4=Article 4 link appearance
    |l5=Article 5 link appearance
    |l6=Article 6 link appearance
    |l7=Article 7 link appearance
    |l8=Article 8 link appearance
    |l9=Article 9 link appearance
    |l10=Article 10 link appearance
    }}
