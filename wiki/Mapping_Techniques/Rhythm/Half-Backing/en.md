# Half-Backing

![](./img/basic.png)

Nominator: Ekaru

Half-Backing is comprised of a quarter beat followed by two-eighth beats, arranged similarly to [Back and Forth Beats](../Back_and_Forth_Beats/) except the third note stops.
This will create a straight line.
