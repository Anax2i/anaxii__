Steps
-----

1) Take a map with horizontal and vertical sliders only

<img src="H15dP_1.jpg" title="H15dP_1.jpg" alt="H15dP_1.jpg" width="600" />

2) Select the current pattern by using Ctrl+A, or manually selecting all the objects by dragging your cursor around them on the timeline.

3) Rotate everything 15° by pressing Ctrl+Shift+R.

<img src="H15dP_2.jpg" title="H15dP_2.jpg" alt="H15dP_2.jpg" width="600" />

4) Done.
