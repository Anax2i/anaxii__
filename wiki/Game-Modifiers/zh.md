“难度调整”是一些可以改变游戏难度的功能，玩家可以任意选择这些 mod 来达到调整难度的目的。一些 mod 可以降低难度，不过也会降低游戏的最终得分。当然，也有一些 mod 可以通过提升游戏难度的方式提升最终的游戏得分。如果玩家使用了 Hidden 或者 Flashlight 这两个 mod 的其中一个（两个都用也行），还可以获得银色 S 以及银色 SS 这些评价。还有一些 mod，是可以进行部分练习的（例如：Relax 这个 mod 可以练习你移动光标的能力）。

在你使用了多个 mod 的时候，得分也会进行乘法形式的加成。比如，在你使用了 Hidden 和 Flashlight 的时候，你会获得 1.1872 倍 (1.06x1.12) = 1.1872 的得分加成。而不是 (1.06+1.12) = 1.18 这样加法形式的加成。由于 osu! 只是将数值精确到 0.01 ，所以只会显示 1.18 得分加成。最近已经将数值使用了四舍五入，用了 Hidden 和 Flashlight 这两个 mod 之后，得分加成已经显示为 1.19 倍， <img src="Soloplay8.png" title="fig:osu!可用的mod列表" alt="osu!可用的mod列表" width="400" />

降低难度的 mod
--------------

这些 mod可以降低游戏的难度，同时也会降低游戏的最后得分。

### <img src="Easy.png" title="fig:Easy.png" alt="Easy.png" width="69" height="65" /> Easy

  
***得分加成：0.5***

***"降低总体难度——更大的圈，HP下降更慢，更低的准确度要求，有3条命。"***

这个mod将会降低游戏的总体难度。也会降低你的最后得分。它可以增大 [圆圈大小](ZH:Circle_size "wikilink")、降低 [缩圈速度](ZH:Approach_rate "wikilink") (AR)、[判定时间](ZH:Overall_difficulty "wikilink") (OD) 以及 [生命消耗](ZH:ife_drain "wikilink")；而且都是较大幅度的降低难度。并且有三条命；当生命值消耗完之后，游戏会短暂暂停以补充生命值，这种情况在每次游戏最多出现两次。在暂停过程中，会进行准备提示（根据 skin 的变化而变化）。不过在重放的情况下，在消耗完生命值之后，不会在重放中暂停，而是会在没有生命值的情况下继续游戏。

然而，这个 mod 在易玩性有一些争议存在。他们认为这个时候圆圈变得异常的大，缩圈也变得非常慢。这个 mod 会式游戏变得更困难读图，在[Insane难度以及一些approved的beatmap中这种情况变得更明显](ZH:Insane "wikilink")。关于这几个问题，可以在These points are elaborated upon in [在这里找到（英文）](http://osu.ppy.sh/forum/viewtopic.php?f=4&t=56606)。[Lybydose](https://osu.ppy.sh/u/64501) 已经发出请求以完善这个 mod。

在[Taiko](ZH:Taiko "wikilink") 模式中，Easy 这个 mod 的作用是降低 [滑条速度](ZH:Slider_velocity "wikilink") （跟 osu! 模式的降低AR相同）、[判定时间](ZH:Overall_difficulty "wikilink") 以及 [生命消耗](ZH:Life_drain "wikilink")。需要注意的是，完成 Spinner 的要求跟 [判定时间](ZH:Overall_difficulty "wikilink") 相关。不像 [Standard](ZH:Standard "wikilink") 和 [Catch The Beat](ZH:Catch_The_Beat "wikilink")，另外在 [Taiko](ZH:Taiko "wikilink") 模式中，使用这个 mod 是可以使生命值填充的更快，而不是进行两次复活。

在 [Catch the Beat](ZH:Catch_The_Beat "wikilink") 模式中，效果跟 osu! 模式相同——水果下落速度变慢（跟 osu! 模式中的降低 AR 相同）、水果变大（跟圆圈变大相同）、有 3 条命。由于只有水平轴，可能会使水果堆在一起，并且有跟 osu! 模式相似的问题出现。

easymod.jpg|很多情况下，Easy 这个 mod 可以在很多地方降低难度。 easymod2.jpg|在一些特殊情况下，Easy 这个 mod 可能会令你头痛。

### <img src="No Fail.png" title="fig:No Fail.png" alt="No Fail.png" width="69" height="65" /> No Fail

  
***得分加成：0.5***

***"不管怎么样，你都失败不了。"***

这个 mod 将会使玩家在 [生命值](ZH:Life_bar "wikilink") 消耗完之后继续游戏。当然，如果玩家使用了 No Fail，却在游戏中什么都不干（说白了就是挂机），游戏依然是算失败的。如果经常使用这个 mod 玩，可能会导致你游戏中的的平均 [准确率](ZH:Accuracy "wikilink") 会大幅度降低。

在[Taiko模式中](ZH:Taiko "wikilink")，No Fail 的作用就是当无法达到游戏所需生命值的时候，依然可以完成游戏。

在[Catch the Beat模式中](ZH:Catch_The_Beat "wikilink")，跟 osu! 模式大致相同。

### <img src="Half-time.png" title="fig:Half-time.png" alt="Half-time.png" width="69" height="65" /> Half Time

<img src="GM_HT.jpg" title="音乐的长度会延长 1/3 ，同时BPM会降低 1/4" alt="音乐的长度会延长 1/3 ，同时BPM会降低 1/4" width="300" />

  
***得分加成：0.3***

***"减&lt;&lt;&lt;&lt;&lt;&lt;速（0.75倍速）"***

Half Time 将会降低音乐的速度，以达到降低难度的目的。不过，BPM 并不是降低到原来的一半，而是原来的75%。这将会使游戏时间延长原音乐长度的25%。这样可能会导致音乐听起来很像“卡顿”，不过人声这部分应该可以过得去。

在 [Taiko](ZH:Taiko "wikilink") 模式中，跟标准的 osu! 模式大致相同，也会降低音乐的速度。可能还会降低物件的密度和降低AR。不过由于在 Taiko 模式中，spinner是计算打击数的，可能会因此增加打击次数。在多人游戏中，将不会改变打击数，因为这样可能会导致最后总分比不用这个 mod 的还要高。而且，如果某张地图的连击数很少，较长的spinner却很多，可以搭配“Hard Rock”（提高spinner的打击数）来“提高”得分。

在 [Catch the Beat](ZH:Catch_The_Beat "wikilink") 模式中，降低 BPM 的行为也跟其他模式一样。并且还会降低玩家控制的人物的速度。因此可能会因为使用了 Half Time 增加一些“Hyperdash”（超级短跑），也因此增加了 Hyperdash 的次数，不过却可以在 Hyperdash 的时候更容易看到下一个水果的位置。

提升难度的mod
-------------

这些mod可以提升游戏的难度，获得更高的可玩性，也可以在多人游戏中获得更高的得分。

### <img src="Hard Rock.png" title="fig:Hard Rock.png" alt="Hard Rock.png" width="69" height="65" /> Hard Rock

<img src="hardrock.jpg" title="Hard Rock在很多情况下都会提升难度。" alt="Hard Rock在很多情况下都会提升难度。" width="400" />

  
***得分加成：1.06 (osu!, Taiko) , 1.12 (Catch the Beat)***

***"各方面的难度都增加一点..."***

Hard Rock（来源于 NDS 游戏：[Elite Beat Agents](ZH:Elite_Beat_Agents "wikilink") 的难度）将会缩小圆圈大小，提升 [判定时间](ZH:Overall_difficulty "wikilink")、[缩圈速度](ZH:Approach_rate "wikilink") 以及 [生命消耗](ZH:HP_drain "wikilink") 两到三个点，直到 10 。需要注意的是，当原来的 AR 已经大于 7 的时候，使用这个 mod 将会使 AR 很接近 10。这个时候，Hard Rock 就会变得很难，在 Insane 难度的情况下更明显（因为还要求有更准确的点击），同时，这个时候，圆圈也可能会一闪而过。并且，这个 mod 也会将地图进行水平轴镜像翻转，所以要有很高的技术才能使用。

在 [Taiko](ZH:Taiko "wikilink") 模式中，Hard Rock 会提升 [滑条速度](slider_velocity "wikilink")，就相当于 [判定时间](overall_difficulty "wikilink")。这个 mod 在 Taiko 模式中比较常见，因为只提升了上述条件的难度。并不要求很精确的点击，尤其是在 Hard 或者 Insane 难度的地图中，这种情况更加明显。需要注意的是，提升 [判定时间](ZH:Overall_difficulty "wikilink") 会提高 Spinner 的打击数。另外，使用 Hard Rock 这个 mod 能够获得比使用 Hidden 这个 mod 高的分数。不过，在很多情况下都提升不了多少，因为每次打击 Spinner 都会获得 300 分。跟普通打击相比，得分少太多了。（在 100 连击以上的情况下，打出一个 GREAT 或者 GOOD 都已经有 550 分。）

在 [Catch the Beat](ZH:Catch_The_Beat "wikilink") 模式中，Hard Rock 也是一个影响的因素比较多因素的 mod。除了会使水果变小（跟缩小圆圈相似）之外，还会加快水果的下落速度（提升 AR），也会有水平轴镜像翻转。在这个时候，可能要有更多的移动甚至是 Hyperdash。由于在这个模式中，Hard Rock 会提升较大的难度，所以得分加成也从 1.06 倍提高到 1.12 倍。

在 <ZH:Osu!mania> 模式中，Hard Rock 并不会影响 note 的位置。只会提高判定时间（OD）和下落速度（AR）。改变 note 位置的，请参见 "Random" 这个 mod（在 Special 栏目中）。

### <img src="Sudden Death.png" title="fig:Sudden Death.png" alt="Sudden Death.png" width="69" height="65" /> Sudden Death

<img src="SD Rarecase.jpeg" title="能够在开启 Sudden Death 中存活并不是一件容易的事情。因为只要漏掉一个 slider tick 都能够将你 K.O. 掉。 通常完成游戏的大概都会这样" alt="能够在开启 Sudden Death 中存活并不是一件容易的事情。因为只要漏掉一个 slider tick 都能够将你 K.O. 掉。 通常完成游戏的大概都会这样" width="200" />

  
***无得分加成***

***"断掉连击就算失败。"***

**Sudden Death 将会使你处于 HP=1 的状态，在断掉 [连击](ZH:Combo "wikilink") 之后马上结束游戏。** 通常情况下，漏掉击打物件、slider 的 tick、没完成 spinner 都会使你断掉连击。但是，漏掉 slider 的尾端依然能保持连击（但是只能获得 100 的得分）。当你下定决心要达到全连的话，打开这个 mod 吧。但是，开启这个 mod 也有可能会造成一定程度的压力，致使影响正常发挥。

Do note there are **rare cases** where the **combo is broken** but if the player managed to **preserve at least a silver of health by getting a slider tick in a very short time-margin right after the combo broke**, the player can continue the map and **no large miss figure will be imposed**.

In [Taiko](Taiko "wikilink") mode, depending on whether you are playing using a mouse or keyboard, you may \[1\] instantly fail the beatmap \[keyboard\] or \[2\] unable to pass the beatmap with no HP gain where all HP is drained \[mouse\]. The second case seems to only happen when you managed to hit some notes using mouse before missing a note during play.

In [Catch the Beat](Catch_the_Beat "wikilink") mode, this mod has the exact same effects as in normal osu! mode, minus the large miss figure.

#### <img src="Perfect.png" title="fig:Perfect.png" alt="Perfect.png" width="69" height="65" /> Perfect

  
***无得分加成***

***在Sudden Death打开的情况下，再点击一遍就能切换到Perfect这个mod***

***"要么SS，要么别开。"***

当你在打开了Sudden Death，然后再次点击，就可以切换到Perfect这个mod。当打开这个mod的时候，任意的“**不准确点击**”都会成为一个“**失误**”，并且会**自动重新开始游戏**。当然，这个mod也是没有得分加成的。只是为了一些SS党的需要，这个mod除了有Sudden Death不能断连击的功能之外，还带有一个只要一个过早/过晚的点击都会自动重新开始，直到你能够从头到尾保持100%准确率或者放弃继续游戏为止。

### <img src="Double time.png" title="fig:Double time.png" alt="Double time.png" width="69" height="65" /> Double Time

<img src="GM_DT.jpg" title="Length of the song is decreased by 1/3 BPM increases by 1/2" alt="Length of the song is decreased by 1/3 BPM increases by 1/2" width="300" />

  
***Score multiplier: 1.12 (osu!, Taiko) , 1.06 (Catch the Beat)***

***"Zoooooooooom"***

Double Time increases the overall beatmap's speed to 150% of the original, reducing the length of the song by 33%. This might be considered deceptive because the BPM is not strictly doubled, despite being called "Double Time". The method used to increase the speed doesn't increase the pitch of vocals (making them allegedly sound like chipmunks) but can make the song sound "muddy".

Because the song is sped up, the [approach rate](approach_rate "wikilink") also increases, which often makes it quite difficult when paired with Hard Rock.

In [Taiko](Taiko "wikilink") mode, the speed is raised and the overall difficulty increased just like in osu! mode. Since the hit leniency is greatly increased on top of Taiko's already strict overall difficulty in addition to making slider ticks a lot harder to hit and reducing the value of spinners, Double Time is usually regarded as the hardest mod in Taiko, and is seldom used.

In [Catch the Beat](Catch_the_Beat "wikilink") mode, the speed and approach rate are changed the same as in other modes. However, since Catch the Beat has no overall difficulty to increase, this mod does nothing more than a BPM and approach rate multiplier and is thus only worth a 1.06x multiplier. Being the opposite of Half Time, though, Double Time greatly decreases the hyperdash leniency, making fruits requiring hyperdashes nigh impossible to catch in some cases.

#### <img src="Nightcore.png" title="fig:Nightcore.png" alt="Nightcore.png" width="69" height="65" /> Nightcore

  
***在Double Time打开的情况下，再点击一遍就能切换到Nightcore这个mod。***

***"洞次打次洞次打次"***

在你打开Double Time的情况下，再点击一遍就能切换到Nightcore这个mod。这个mod将会提高人声的音调，同时也会为音乐加上一段固定频率的低音节拍。得分加成跟Double Time一致。当然，打开这个mod的同时，也**<u>默认打开了Double Time这个mod</u>**。

### <img src="Hidden.png" title="fig:Hidden.png" alt="Hidden.png" width="69" height="65" /> Hidden

  
***Score multiplier: 1.06*** (osu!,Taiko,CtB) , ***1.00*** (osu!mania)

***"Play with no approach circles and fading notes for a slight score advantage."***

Hidden debuted in Ouendan 2, the second Japanese DS game. In osu!, it eliminates the approach circles and causing the hit circles to vanish shortly after appearing, forcing players to more or less memorize the timing and, to a lesser degree, placement. Numbers on sliders and their approach circles vanish, but the sliders themselves do not, so the player only need remember the timing and which end to click.

In [Taiko](Taiko "wikilink") mode, the notes fade out about halfway through the screen, requiring that the player not only memorize their timing and placement but their color too. However, sliders and spinners still cross the full timeline and remain visible, except that the spinner has no spinner circle to indicate when it times out. On maps with high overall difficulty, Hidden is used by experienced players to increase score instead of Hard Rock.

In [Catch the Beat](Catch_the_Beat "wikilink") mode, Hidden causes notes to disappear halfway down the screen. This is the most commonly used mod in Catch the Beat, since the other difficulty increasing mods either greatly increase approach rate (DT and HR) or require heavy memorization (Flashlight).

In [osu!mania](osu!mania "wikilink") mode, Hidden can be used upon pressing Fade In mod again. It is an inverse of Fade In where the below of the conveyor is blocked instead. Your Judgement bar will be blocked too but not your colourful keys.

***Score multiplier: 1.00*** (osu!mania only)

This mod is specially for osu!mania only. It hides about 1/4 vision dead above the conveyor. The darkness will spread until the 500th combo count where 3/4 of the conveyor will be blocked until you break the combo streak. At 300th combo count, it is 1/2 of the conveyor.

### <img src="Flashlight.png" title="fig:Flashlight.png" alt="Flashlight.png" width="69" height="65" /> Flashlight

  
***Score multiplier: 1.12***

***"Restricted view area."***

Flashlight reduces the visible area of the screen to a small, illuminated section around the cursor. The visible area becomes smaller at 100 combo and again at 200 combo, returning to its original size if the combo is broken. This mod has been subject to heavy controversy in the past, as its implementation makes it the easiest mod for hackers to cheat on. This mod has been made unranked before during a time of heavy cheater activity, but was restored not long after.

In [Taiko](Taiko "wikilink") mode, the position of visible area is fixed at the hitting area. The visible area shrinks at the same combo intervals as in standard osu!. Theoretically, Flashlight does not require memorization for every map, as unlike in the other two modes, all notes are guaranteed to be visible at some point in time. However, except on songs with a very low BPM or low combo, human reaction times generally aren't adequate for such a feat. When paired with Hidden, the flashlight size barely matters, as the notes are completely invisible at any combo.

In [Catch the Beat](Catch_the_Beat "wikilink") mode, the flashlight is the same, except that it follows the character. The visible area is greater than in standard osu! or in Taiko, but is still quite a challenge as it disables you entirely from seeing notes on the top or the other side of the screen. When paired with Hidden, fruits are visible momentarily if you are directly under them until you reach 100 combo, at which point they become completely invisible.

Special Mods
------------

The mods listed below can't be used in conjunction with each other, except for Relax and Spun Out. Additionally, Auto can't be used along with Sudden Death/Perfect.

### <img src="Relax.png" title="fig:Relax.png" alt="Relax.png" width="69" height="65" /> Relax

  
***Score multiplier: 0 (Unranked)***

***"You don't need to click.
Give your clicking/tapping finger a break from the heat of things.
\*\*UNRANKED\*\*"***

A mod in which you don't need to click. Simply hover your cursor over each [hit circle](Hit_Objects#Hit_Circle "wikilink") and it will be hit with the correct timing automatically. Using this mod also hides the score, combo and misses (except in Taiko) and makes it impossible to fail the map, much like No Fail. Also, your spin/min value will be in x2 value, in which completing certain spinners becomes slightly easier.

In [Taiko](Taiko "wikilink") mode, the correct color for the notes is hit regardless of the button pressed, sliders do not stop you from hitting slider ticks if you hit too fast, and spinners can be cleared with any button sequence instead of alternating blue and red.

In [Catch the Beat](Catch_the_Beat "wikilink") mode, this mod allows the character to be moved freely at any speed with the mouse.

### <img src="AutoPilot.png" title="fig:AutoPilot.png" alt="AutoPilot.png" width="69" height="65" /> Auto Pilot

  
***Score multiplier: 0 (Unranked, osu! only)***

***"Automatic cursor movement - just follow the rhythm.
\*\*UNRANKED\*\*"***

A mod in which you only need to click. The game moves the cursor for you so you can focus on clicking with the correct timing. Using this mod also hides the score, combo and misses, and makes it impossible to fail the map, much like No Fail.

Auto Pilot is not available for use in [Taiko](Taiko "wikilink") or [Catch the Beat](Catch_the_Beat "wikilink") modes.

### <img src="Spun Out.png" title="fig:Spun Out.png" alt="Spun Out.png" width="69" height="65" /> Spun Out

  
***得分加成： 0.9 ( 只在osu模式下有效）***

***"转盘将会自动完成"***

Spun Out 是一个在游戏中为你旋转转盘的mod，该mod允许你在map中休息 而不是使你的手臂疲倦。

Spun Out 在[Taiko或](Taiko "wikilink")[Catch the Beat](Catch_the_Beat "wikilink") 模式中无效。

### <img src="Autoplay.png" title="fig:Autoplay.png" alt="Autoplay.png" width="69" height="65" /> Auto

  
***"Watch a perfect automated play through the song"***

A mod that allows the player to watch a perfect playthrough of the selected beatmap. This mod functions exactly like a [replay](replay "wikilink"), allowing 2x speed to be used and comments on the beatmap to be seen and made. The Auto mod will always get an SS, except in special cases. These cases are all noted as unrankable designs and with the exception of ninja [spinners](spinners "wikilink") in some old maps, will never occur in current ranked beatmaps.

In [Taiko](Taiko "wikilink") mode, the Auto mod will do a perfect playthrough, except that occasionally it will miss a tick on sliders that do not start on a whole or half beat and will not always be able to complete spinners on maps with high overall difficulty.

In [Catch the Beat](Catch_the_Beat "wikilink") mode, the Auto mod will actually go beyond perfect and use unlimited speed to catch every fruit in a spinner, despite this being literally impossible for a player.

### <img src="Cinema.png" title="fig:Cinema.png" alt="Cinema.png" width="69" height="65" /> Cinema

<img src="GM_Cinema_comp.jpg" title="Auto和Cinema mod之间的比较" alt="Auto和Cinema mod之间的比较" width="200" />

  
***"按Auto两下就会切换到Cinema mod"***

这mod只会显示背景图、故事模板(Storyboard)和视频，其他hitcircle神马的鬼东东都会隐藏掉。你可以开启HT/DT/NC mod和听见该难度的音效。但是，你不可以暂停还有只显示"pass"的故事模板而已。背景暗化会自动调成0%，视频和故事模板会自动开启(即使你在设定中没开启这些特效)。

***Score multiplier: 0.78~1.0 (osu!mania only)***

Inverse Mods
------------

Not all mods can be applied at the same time. Some mods are the inverse of others, and will be deactivated if their inverse mod is selected.

These are a few notable examples:-

-   Hard Rock is the inverse of Easy
-   Sudden Death/Perfect is the inverse of No Fail and any unranked mods.
    -   Unranked mods: Relax, Auto-Pilot, Auto.
-   Double Time is the inverse of Half Time

Unranked Mods
-------------

All of the special mods (except Spun Out) will cause any plays with them activated to be unranked and ineligible for addition to ranked or online stats. However, exporting a replay of one of these scores and loading it up into your game will cause them to appear on your local scoreboard.

In [Multi-play](Multiplayer "wikilink"), Tag Co-Op and Tag Team VS are under this category because it was not done by the skill of a player but as a whole group of players cooperation. Despite it is an unranked mod, player will still get a increase in total score by their own individual score rather than their group/team score.
